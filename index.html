About the Artist

I am Fatima Shoukat, known to most as artfsy_craftfsy. What began as a college jest “Artsy & Craftsy” — evolved into an identity I own. My affair with painting began in childhood, borrowing my sister’s watercolours. For a season, I turned to cooking, yet art always returned, no longer pastime but solace. Acrylics lend me swiftness, oils grant me patience; together, they balance my hand.

At heart, my aim is simple: to take what is ordinary — a face, a moment, a fragment of life and render it into something that lingers. If my art leaves you comforted, nostalgic, or quietly stirred, then it has spoken as it ought.

Timeline

Art doesn’t come with overnight shipping. It matures — like fine wine, like ideas, like the perfect shade of red. Here’s how long brilliance usually takes:

Custom Pieces: 2–4 weeks — Because beauty insists on patience.
Digital Art: 1–2 weeks — The closest thing to instant gratification I offer.
Portraits: 3–5 weeks — Faces deserve reverence. Yours, especially.
Rush Jobs: +50% — Genius on demand comes at a price, and with extra caffeine.
